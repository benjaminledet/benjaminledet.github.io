Services & pricing

Flash audit

Design

The migration is the right moment to rework the design system, or even to change your visual identity. The components get rewritten anyway, so you might as well decide now what they become. Deciding later means touching every screen again. The overhaul is real work, especially on the design side, and it is priced in the proposal.

That is what we did at Yescapa, where we used the migration for a full redesign of the app, co-led with our designer. Six years working side by side taught me to talk design as much as architecture.
